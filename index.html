NHS Guidance

Aligned with the patterns your midwife looks for.

5-1-1

5-1-1 Contraction Pattern

Contractions every 5 minutes, lasting around 1 minute, for at least 45 minutes. Based on NHS guidelines, this is a good time to call your midwife or maternity unit.

4-1-1

Contraction Frequency Increasing

Contractions are becoming more frequent. Based on general guidelines, if you haven't already, contact your midwife or maternity unit and make sure your hospital bag is ready.

3-1-1

High Frequency Contraction Pattern

Contractions are very frequent. Based on general NHS guidelines, this pattern is associated with active labour. Contact your midwife or maternity unit if you haven't already.
